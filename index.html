Privacy Policy

At NotifyHub, safeguarding your privacy is our top priority. This document explains in clear terms how we handle your personal information within our application.

Saving Notifications

We want to assure you that we do not retain any notifications or user data on our servers. When you use the "NotifyHub" application, incoming notifications are saved directly on your device. These notifications are stored locally and can only be accessed on the specific device where the application is installed. Rest assured that your notifications remain confidential and are not transmitted to any external servers.

Storage

In order to preserve notifications and associated media, our application requests access to your device's storage. It's essential to understand that this permission is exclusively utilized for the purpose of storing pertinent data. We want to reassure you that our application abstains from accessing any files or data unrelated to its fundamental functionality.

Third-Party Services

To enhance the quality of our service, our application collaborates with various third-party providers. These providers independently gather their own data. We encourage you to review their respective privacy policies to ensure your comfort with their data collection practices.

Advertising

Our free Android application may include advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These advertisements are essential for supporting our free service and ensuring its continued availability to users. For comprehensive details regarding Google AdMob's policies and practices, we encourage you to review their official policies by visiting: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites?hl=en.

Firebase

Firebase Google Analytics

This powerful tool enables us to gather comprehensive data on user interactions and behavior within the application, helping us make informed decisions to improve functionality.

Firebase Remote Config

With Firebase Remote Config, we can dynamically adjust the behavior and appearance of our application without requiring users to download updates. This allows us to optimize the user experience in real-time.

Firebase Crashlytics

Identifying and resolving issues promptly is vital for ensuring a smooth user experience. Firebase Crashlytics provides valuable insights into app crashes and errors, allowing us to address them swiftly and effectively.

Data Collected

In line with Firebase's data collection protocols, specific categories of information may be automatically gathered to enhance the application's functionality. These encompass, but are not limited to, mobile ad identifiers, device IDs (IDFV/Android ID), instance IDs, and analytics app instance IDs. This collected data is solely utilized to improve user experience and provide enhanced services in accordance with our privacy policies. https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.
